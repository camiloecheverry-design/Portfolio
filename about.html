What sets me apart

I don't just make it look good. I make it make sense.

Plenty of designers can push pixels. I start from the problem instead: I dig into the brief, map the real user journey, and pressure-test decisions against data and technical limits before polishing a single screen.

Research & data, not guessworkI validate with usability testing, A/B testing, and analytics, and let the findings reshape the design.
End-to-end ownershipFrom problem definition through high-fidelity UI and developer handoff, I see the work all the way to production.
